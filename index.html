About

My Journey

Tech has fascinated me ever since childhood. I was enamored by video games and the satisfaction of navigating through their many menus and functions. The day I received my first smartphone I spent hours trying out different designs and layouts to create an efficient user flow. It was that day I realized I was passionate about technology. After graduating from UC San Diego with a B.S. in Cognitive Science w/ Spec. in Design and Interaction, I now spend my time programming full-stack projects. As I continue to learn more about tech, my vision for my career is to be part of a team that both trailblazes the field of product design and software development and mentors future generations of creators.
